Welcome to Greer Dental

Greer Dental is a family dental practice that has been caring for patients in the Minot area for over fifty five years. Our team of three dentists and ten staff members strive to provide quality dental care in a modern relaxed environment. We treat patients of all ages and offer a broad range of dental services to custom tailor treatment for each patient's needs and expectations. We look forward to meeting you and are now accepting new patients.


Dr. Murray Greer

Dr. Greer did his undergraduate training at the University of Wisconsin Eau Claire and received his DDS degree from the University of Minnesota. He married his wife, Dr. Beth Greer while attending dental school and they both graduated in 1986. Following dental school, Dr. Greer did a one year General Practice Residency at the University of Washington Dental School in Seattle Washington. He and his wife Beth then moved to Minot in 1987 where they both went into practice with Beth's father, Dr. O. Richard Nutter. Dr. Greer is a member of the Minot Dental Society, the NW District Dental Society, the North Dakota Dental Association, and the American Dental Association. He was president of the North Dakota Dental Association in 2013-2014 and was inducted into the International College of Dentists in 2015.

Dr. Stephanie Ness

Dr. Stephanie grew up in Beaverton, Oregon, a suburb of Portland. She graduated from Seattle Pacific Universtiy in Seattle, Washington with a Bachelor of Arts degree in biology and a chemistry minor. She earned her Doctor of Dental Medicine (DMD) degree from Oregon Health and Science University in 2013. She and Dr. Alex were married two days after their dental school graduation. She is a member of the American Dental association, the North Dakota Dental Association, the Minot Dental Society, and the Academy of General Dentistry. In her free time, Dr. Stephanie enjoys cooking, traveling, and singing with the Minot Chamber Chorale.

Dr. Alex Ness

Dr. Alex grew up in Minot and graduated from Our Redeemer's Christian School. He completed his undergraduate degree at North Dakota State University in Fargo, earning a Bachelor of Science degree in zoology and a minor in chemistry. He then attended Oregon Health and Science University in Portland, Oregon, earning his DMD in 2013. It was there that he met and married Dr. Stephanie. He is a member of the American Dental association, the North Dakota Dental Association, the Minot Dental Society, and the Academy of General Dentistry. Outside of the Office, Dr. Alex enjoys hunting, watching the occasional TV show, and spending time with family.
